Why Participate

A Momentous Festival
to Invoke His Grace

As part of the Chinmaya Amrit Mahotsav — marking 75 glorious years of Chinmaya Mission — Chinmaya Mission Chicago invites all devotees to this Grand Hanumān Chalisā Yagna on July 11, 2026 at the DuPage County Fairgrounds, Wheaton, IL. According to our ancient traditions, samashti upāsanā — collective prayer — multiplies in power manyfold. Join 1,008 voices in unified chant, creating an atmosphere of bhakti that blesses our entire community.

🚗 Getting There

Parking: Free on-site parking available. Arrive early.

By Car: Take I-290 W to I-88 W, exit Naperville Rd, head north to Manchester Rd.

By Metra: Union Pacific West Line — Wheaton Station, then rideshare.

From Chicago: Approximately 35–45 minutes by car.
