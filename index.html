About this Yagna

As part of the Chinmaya Amrit Mahotsav — marking 75 years of Chinmaya Mission — we invite all Chicagoland devotees to this Grand Hanumān Chalisā Yagna. According to our ancient traditions, samashti upāsanā — collective prayer — multiplies in power. Join thousands of voices in a unified chant, creating an atmosphere of bhakti that blesses our entire community.
